ABOUT US

Empowering Investor with Real-World Assets (RWA's)

REBEL is an accessible ecosystem that empowers investors in the Real-World Asset (RWA) Market. Through our omni-chain Platform, investors gain transparency and trust when investing into diverse assets starting with gold, silver, platinum and palladium and thereafter expanding into other asset classes. The REBEL Platform offers access to tokenised assets in unit sizes that are affordable and made available to anyone, anywhere, democratising accessibility and providing easily tradable and liquid positions, that are instantly settled and securely recorded on-chain.

The REBEL Platform enables a more stable and secure option for market participants seeking stability, while enjoying growth opportunity simultaneously.

Own Real-World Assets

REBEL’s RWA Yield Vaults allow holders to digitally vault their RWA tokens, such as gold, for a specified period . In order to vault RWA tokens, a corresponding and required amount of REBEL's utility token

Legacy Meets Liquidity : A token that represents ownership of real world assets (RWA) that have historical value (legacy) and ease of trading (liquidity) on the digital market. This token is designed to combine the security and stability of traditional assets with the accessibility and efficiency of blockchain technology. Thus, “Legacy Meets Liquidity” presents a new way to leverage valuable assets such as property, art or other financial instruments in the digital financial ecosystem, enabling greater liquidity and global accessibility without reducing the value or security of those assets. The emphasis on the concept of “Legacy Meets Liquidity” is to enable investors to leverage the advantages of blockchain technology in terms of ownership recording, transparency, and global accessibility while maintaining the traditional value characteristics of the underlying physical or financial asset.
